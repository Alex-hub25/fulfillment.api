What is Packit?

Packit is a fully automated fulfillment system for small and and medium size companies. Providing seamless flow betwween customer orders to setting up freight carriers.

What is the process?

  1. Scan it

    2. Packit products include a module that handles all of heavy lifting. Simply attach the module near your scale, connect the five pin connector to the scale monitor, and set your package down with the packing slip facing the camera.

  2. Log it

    3. Packit's module will upload all of the shipping, product, and dimensional data to a secure database for easy record keeping and information recovery.

  3. Print it

    4. Packit will concurently provide custom documents for international Shipments while setting up pickup from your prefered carriers at the best possible pricing.
